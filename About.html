Welcome to Lagos Continental Hotel

  • The Lagos Continental Hotel, is a 5-Star Hotel located at Plot 52A, Kofo Abayomi Street, Victoria Island, within the heart of the central business district of Lagos Nigeria. Our prime location makes the Hotel ideal for both business and leisure stays.
  • Our Hotel has 23 floors with 358 rooms and suites, making the Hotel the tallest in West Africa, with an amazing view of the Atlantic Ocean, Lagos creek and the city.
  • The International, Airport- Muritala Muhammed International Airport is about 28.9 Km drive to the Hotel. Embassies, museums, leisure centers, shopping malls and local markets are within the proximity of the Hotel.
  • The Hotel has a 1400 sqm Grand African Ballroom and six smaller meeting / break out rooms.
  • The Recreation Center located on the 5th floor houses an Infinity Pool, Beauty Spa, Hair Saloon and Health & fitness center. The gym operates 24/7.
  • Six Food and Beverage outlets which offer National, Continental and Asian Cuisine.
